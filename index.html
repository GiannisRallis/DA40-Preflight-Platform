01 · FEATURES

Everything a flight school needs

Built specifically for the DA-40 D. Every calculation follows the POH exactly.

⚖️
Weight & Balance (§6.4.3)
Full 7-row W&B table exactly as in the POH. Calculates mass, moment and CG in both metric (kg/m) and imperial (lb/in) units simultaneously.
→ Rows 1–7 · kg·m & in·lb · Live CG
📐
CG Envelope Diagram (6.4.4)
Live SVG envelope diagram plots Row 5 and Row 7 points against the actual POH limits. Instant visual feedback — green dot = within limits.
→ Normal & Utility category · Forward limit curve
🛫
Performance Calculator
Interpolates T/O roll, T/O over 50ft, LDG roll, ROC and density altitude from digitized POH performance charts. Corrects for temperature, runway surface and wind.
→ ISA correction · Surface factors · Wind adj.
👨‍✈️
Instructor Dashboard
Instructors see all student submissions filtered by name, status or date. Click any row to open the full detail modal and leave comments that appear in the student's history.
→ Modal review · Status workflow · Notes
📊
Analytics & Statistics
CG histogram, common error tracking with pass rates, per-student performance rankings. Identify which students need the most attention at a glance.
→ CG distribution · Pass rate · Student ranking
📄
PDF Export
One-click professional PDF with the complete W&B table, CG results and performance section. Formatted for A4, ready for the physical flight folder.
→ A4 PDF · Both units · Bilingual (EL/EN)
🌐
Bilingual — EL / EN
Every label, table header, status message and PDF output is available in both Greek and English. Switches instantly without a page reload via localStorage.
→ Dynamic switch · Persists across sessions
🔒
Self-Hosted & Secure
Runs entirely on your own server. JWT authentication, Row Level Security on every table — students only see their own data. No third-party cloud required.
→ Docker Compose · GoTrue Auth · RLS
💾
Backup & Restore
One-command database backup with automatic gzip compression. Keeps the last 30 backups by default. Works on both Windows (PowerShell) and Linux.
→ .sql.gz · Auto-rotate · PS1 + SH
02 · WORKFLOW

From paper form to digital record
in under 5 minutes

01
Student opens the Preflight Calculator
Fills in empty mass/arm from the aircraft's M&B Report, passengers, baggage and fuel. The system calculates Row 5 and Row 7 automatically with live CG verification against the envelope.
W&B + Performance in one form
02
Performance section calculated
Student enters pressure altitude, OAT, wind and runway surface. T/O roll, LDG roll, ROC and density altitude are interpolated from digitized POH tables with ISA and surface corrections.
Interpolated · Corrected · Instant
03
Submit for instructor review
One click saves the entire preflight to the database with status "Submitted". The instructor dashboard shows a pending badge immediately. Student can export a PDF at any point.
status: submitted → reviewed
04
Instructor reviews and annotates
Instructor opens the submission, sees all W&B and performance data in a full detail modal. Leaves written comments and marks as reviewed. Notes appear instantly in the student's history.
Modal review · Notes · Analytics
